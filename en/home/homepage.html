lfaprivativo is a winery in the beautiful Umbria, where passion for the land and respect for nature have met in the production of natural wines. The idea that guides the creation of our wines is the simplification of agricultural and oenological processes, with the must to do not forgive any defect. The company's mission is to bring to the bottle a concept of authenticity, where the wine is left free to express itself, a messenger of seasonality and territory without the oppression of chemistry. Harvesting is done manually, carefully selecting the best bunches of grapes to ensure the highest quality.
“Transparency and Authenticity”
Transparency is our commitment. We want every consumer to know the story behind every bottle, from the vineyards to the winery. We pride ourselves on our authenticity and believe that clarity about our production process helps create a genuine bond with those who appreciate our wines.
“Craftsmanship and Tradition”
Each bottle of wine is the result of meticulous craftsmanship and a deep respect for winemaking traditions. We recognize the beauty of tradition and combine it with a touch of innovation, which has always guided our engineering paths.
Environmental Sustainability
We are committed to sustainable and responsible agricultural practices. We avoid the use of pesticides and chemicals, favoring natural solutions to protect our vineyards.

Who we are

Alex and Simone, two friends who since childhood have shared sports, academic paths and passions, including that for nature and wine.

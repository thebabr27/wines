Name

lfaprivativo is the prefix used in the Italian language with the meaning of negation. The choice of the name is led by our desire to negate and question the current concept of wine, which is increasingly standardized to elegant chemical mixture homologated to classic lines of taste. In addition, the name is also meant to highlight that we have both "negated," or rather revised, what has been our life path so far, replacing stretches of engineering with work in the vineyard and winery.

People

lex and Simone, two friends who since childhood have shared sports, academic paths and passions, including that for nature and wine. The Alfaprivativo project was born in 2021 as a desire to bring a concept of authenticity and passion to the bottle, starting again from the traditions of our grandparents.

Natural Approach

hat is a Natural wine? For us, wine is conceived in the vineyard, all the efforts of the vine, which for months puts all of itself into those few bunches of grapes, inspire our ideas So that the best product is made with what the season has offered us. In the wine cellar, on the other hand, the wine comes to light with the most meticulous attention and avoiding any kind of intervention that would characterize its natural qualities. Therefore. NO to: Selected yeasts, temperature control, filtrations, clarifications, additions of any rectifying substance. Natural, or "au natural," means without additions, without tricks, corrections or cosmetics of any kind. Natural means having the courage to show oneself for what one is. Conventional or Natural for us, wine must be clean, forthright. A flawed wine, is simply a flawed wine, calling it Natural is just an excuse of those who have made some mistakes.

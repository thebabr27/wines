ORIOR

Umbria Bianco
Orior from latin means to rise, grow; the choice is to celebrate the abandoned vineyard we restored.

Product details

  • Name: Orior.

  • Category:  Orange wine, natural in winery and organic in vineyards.

  • Grapes varieties: 40% Trebbiano Toscano, 20% Malvasia bianca lunga, 10% San Colombano, 10% Alionza .

  • Vinification: Harvest on penultimate week of September, with different stages of ripening, grapes were sweeter. Spontaneous fermentation with fermentation foot. Must was in contact 15 days on its skins with two fullings per day, followed by very soft pressing, 7 months in stainless steel. NO filtration, NO added sulphites.

  • Recommendation for use:  Especially suitable in all seasons and in any case of thirst. Serving temperature 12-14Â°C. Alcohol 12,5% vol. After opening, leave few moments breathing, is alive product.

  • Funny fact: The varieties San Colombano and Alionza are unknown in our area and almost unique. The farmer who planted the vineyard, simply asked for white grapes without knowing he received fantastic varieties.

Tasting notes

  • At the nose: Warm and fruity, vanilla, sweet peach and a soft touch of smoke in the closure.

  • In the mouth:  Funny and joyous game of the varieties, soils and ripening that alternate themselves between fresh sensation given by the acidity of Trebbiano and sweet complexity of the full body of San Colombano and Alionza, closing with a fruity sapidity of Malvasia.

TASCAIOSO

Umbria Rosso I.G.T.
Tascaioso in the dialect of Morano Osteria means something gritty, pugnacious, strong-willed, unwilling to back down from its intention despite circumstances.

Product details

  • Name: Orior.

  • Category:  Red wine, natural in winery and organic in vineyards.

  • Grapes varieties:  80%Sangiovese old clones, 15%Ciliegiolo, 5%Merlot .

  • Vinification: Harvest on half of September, spontaneous fermentation with fermentation foot. Must was in contact 21days on its skins with two fullings per day, followed by very soft pressing, 9 months in stainless steel. NO filtration, NO added sulphites.

  • Recommendation for use:  Especially good for dinners, lunches, breakfasts, snacks, goals and disappointments, cruel world, in short: always! Serving temperature 14-16°C. Alcohol 13,5% vol. After opening, leave few moments breathing, is alive product.

  • Funny fact:  : Next to the vineyard there are a lot of wild sour-cherry trees, the indigenous yeasts on the grapes that have determined the wine profile, are the same of these trees, that’s why the uncommon note of sour cherry in this variety.

Tasting notes

  • At the nose: Red ripe fruit jam, Sour cherry, St. John's Wort flowers, balsamic, intense almost wild.

  • In the mouth:  Immediate explosion of wild sour cherry, the freshness given by an important acidity is perfectly balanced by a warm hug of sweet notes of rip fruits.
